Quick start

Get him onto the glass

  1. Install the app. Open clawdpad-poke.apk and tap Install / Update. It updates over any previous ClawdPad and keeps your data. 
    termux-setup-storage        # once — grants file access
termux-open ~/storage/downloads/clawdpad-poke.apk
  2. Wake the block. Power on the Lightpad Block and turn Bluetooth on.
  3. Connect. Open ClawdPad and tap 🔌 connect to my block. It shows “looking for your block…”, links up, and CLAWD appears on the pad. The Keeper holds the link and auto-reconnects if the block drops or sleeps.

The controls

The glass is the controller

Every mode reads your touch as one of four gestures. When you start a mode, the app’s status line reminds you which ones it uses.

TAPa quick press-and-release
SWIPEpress and slide across the pad
HOLDpress and keep your finger down
SCRUBdrag back-and-forth or in a circle — used to “charge”

The main event

🔗Poképad — snap to battle

Snap a second block onto the first and a fully autonomous Gen-III Pokémon battle begins — one full creature per block, no human control.

They fight by the real mechanics: authentic base stats across all 386 species, the real 17-type chart (fire melts grass, water douses fire, ground shrugs off electric), the damage formula, crits, and a move-picking AI on both sides.

Watch the beats: a Poké-Ball drops and bursts open to summon each mon, the attacker lunges with an impact streak, the defender flashes and recoils, and a knocked-out mon greys out, tips over, and sinks away. HP bars ride the top of each block; a banner calls the action — SUPER!, RESIST, the move name. Pull the blocks apart to end it.

If something’s off

Troubleshooting

The block won’t connectMake sure it’s powered and Bluetooth is on, toggle Bluetooth, and tap 🔌 connect to my block again — the Keeper keeps retrying.
“connect his block first”You started a mode before connecting. Connect the block, then open the mode.
Snapping doesn’t start a battleConnect the first block, then physically snap the second onto it — you’ll see 🔗 SNAP! and the matchup. Pulling them apart ends it.
“App not installed” on updateA signature mismatch. Long-press the old ClawdPad icon → Uninstall, then install fresh.
Music mode does nothingGrant the audio permission, then tap dance again.
